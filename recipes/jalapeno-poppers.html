Air Fryer Jalepeño Poppers

Description

These Air Fryer Jalapeño Poppers come out so good and I can make them relatively quickly in the air fryer. They are creamy and slightly crunchy. The perfect combination! So if you are looking for an amazing air fryer appetizer recipe then you really gotta try these jalapeño poppers!

Ingredients

  • 6 large jalapeños
  • 1 tablespoon olive oil
  • 8 ounce block cream cheese
  • 1 teaspoon garlic powder
  • 1/4 teaspoon salt
  • 1/4 teaspoon black pepper
  • 3 tablespoons sliced green onions
  • 1 cup shredded Colby Jack cheese
  • 1/2 pound bacon, cooked and chopped

Instructions

  1. Slice jalapeños lengthwise. Remove seeds and veins. Drizzle olive oil over jalapeno slices and rub them to thoroughly coat.
  2. Spray air fryer basked with cooking spray. Place jalapeño halves into air fryer basket.
  3. Air fry jalapeño halves at 400ºF for 4 minutes. (Air fryer does not need to be preheated first.) Let cool 5 minutes or until cool enough to handle.
  4. In a bowl, mix together cream cheese, garlic powder, onion powder, salt and pepper until smooth.
  5. Stir in 1/2 cup shredded cheese, chopped bacon, and sliced green onions.
  6. Fill all jalapeño halves evenly with the cream cheese mixture.
  7. Place all the jalapeños in an even layer inside the air fryer basket.
  8. Air fry at 400ºF for 5 minutes.
  9. Serve and enjoy!
