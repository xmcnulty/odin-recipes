Lasagna

Description

This recipe has simple ingredients but tons of flafor with a creamy ricotta mixture, savory meat sauce, and tons of mozzrella cheese.

This is a great freezer meal, make-ahead recipe, and leftovers freeze and reheat super well.

Ingredients

Cheese Filling
  • 2 cups ricotta cheese
  • 1 large egg
  • 2 cups mozzarella cheese
  • 3/4 cup parmesan cheese, freshly grated
  • 1/2 teaspoon salt
  • 1/4 tsp pepper
Meat Sauce
  • 1 tablespoon olive oil
  • 1 yellow onion, finely diced
  • 3/4 lb. ground beef
  • 3/4 lb. ground Italian sausage
  • 3 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1/2 cup chicken broth
  • 40 oz. marinara sauce
  • 1 tablespoon tomato paste
  • 1 teaspoon hot sauce
  • 1 teaspoon Worcestershire sauce
Lasagna Noodles & Cheese Topping
  • 12 lasagna noodles
  • 2.5 cups mozzarella cheese

Instructions

Prep Work
  1. Combine the cheese filling ingredients in a medium bowl and set aside.
Make the Meat Sauce
  1. Heat olive oil over medium heat and add the diced onions. Let them soften for 10 mintues.
  2. Add the ground beef and sausage and increase heat to medium high. Cook 8-10 minutes, or until cooked through.
  3. Add the chicken broth and use a spatula to "clean" the bottom and sides of the pot.
  4. Add the marinara sauce, tomato paste, hot sauce, and Worcestershire sauce. Bring to a boild, then reduce to a simmer. Cover partially and let the sauce simmer gently while you boil the pasta noodles.
Preheat Oven and Cook the Lasagna Noodles
  1. Preheat oven to 375 degrees.
  2. Begin boiling a large pot of salted pasta water for the lasagna noodles. Once boiling, cook the noodles to al dente.
  3. Lay the cooked noodles flat on wax or parchment paper while you assemble the lasagna.
Assemble
  1. Spread 1 heaping cup of meat sauce on the bottom of a 9x13-inch casserole dish.
  2. Add 4 lasagna noodles, overlapping them if needed.
  3. Spread 1/3 of the cheese mixture over the noodles, followed by 1 1/2 cups of the meat sauce.
  4. Top with 4 more lasagna noodles, 1/3 of the cheese mix, 1 1/2 cups meat sauce.
  5. Finish with 4 more lasagna noodles, the rest of the cheese mixture, and the rest of the meat sauce.
  6. Top with 2 1/2 cups of Mozzarella cheese.
  7. Spray foil with nonstick cooking spray and place it spray-side-down on the top of the baking dish.
Bake
  1. Bake for 35 minutes. Remove cover and bake for 10 more minutes.
  2. Remove and garnish with fresh parsley. Let it sit for 15 minutes prior to serving.
